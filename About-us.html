Our Story

Healing With Care at - Vitalora Hospital

Vitalora Hospital was established with a mission to provide world-class healthcare that is both accessible and compassionate. We are dedicated to improving lives through advanced medical expertise, modern facilities, and a patient-first approach.

From preventive check-ups and diagnostics to specialized treatments and emergency care, Vitalora Hospital brings together expert doctors, skilled nurses, and cutting-edge technology to ensure the highest standards of care for every patient.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in healthcare, serving families with integrity, empathy, and innovation. At Vitalora, we believe in healing beyond medicine — creating a healthier and happier community for generations to come.

Our Healthcare Mission & Vision

VISION

To become a trusted leader in healthcare, recognized for providing compassionate, high-quality, and innovative medical care. We strive to enhance the health, well-being, and quality of life of every patient, while setting new standards of excellence in patient safety, medical expertise, and community-focused healthcare services.

To deliver patient-centered healthcare through highly skilled medical professionals, state-of-the-art technology, and a commitment to excellence. Our Community & CSR Journey

2018 – Free Health Camps

Launched our first free health check-ups in rural areas, reaching families with basic medical care and spreading awareness on hygiene and preventive healthcare.

2020 – Blood Donation Drives

Launched extensive blood donation drives across multiple cities, encouraging volunteers to contribute and ensuring a steady supply of blood for emergency treatments and critical patients.

2022 – Awareness Programs

Introduced community-wide initiatives on hygiene, nutrition, and mental health, empowering underprivileged families with knowledge and practices to improve their daily lives and overall well-being.

2024 – Rural Health Outreach

Started mobile clinic services delivering essential healthcare, medicines, and regular checkups to remote villages, ensuring medical support reaches those with limited access to hospitals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Visiting hours are from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM. Visitors are requested to follow safety and hygiene protocols.

Appointments can be booked online through our website or by calling the hospital reception desk. Walk-ins are also available for general consultations.

Our hospital provides outpatient and inpatient care, emergency services, diagnostic facilities, surgeries, and specialized departments such as cardiology, pediatrics, orthopedics, and gynecology. We aim to deliver complete healthcare solutions under one roof.

Yes, our emergency department and ambulance services operate 24/7. A dedicated team of doctors and nurses is always available to handle urgent and critical cases.

We accept most leading health insurance providers for both cashless treatment and reimbursement. For confirmation, please contact our insurance help desk before admission.

Yes, our hospital has a fully stocked in-house pharmacy that operates 24/7. Patients and visitors can easily access prescribed medicines and essential health products without leaving the hospital premises. The pharmacy ensures availability of genuine medicines, timely service, and support from qualified pharmacists.

Bills can be paid through cash, debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, or insurance (cashless facility). Our billing counter is open 24/7 for patient convenience.

Yes, we provide cashless hospitalization for patients insured with our partner insurance providers. Please visit the insurance help desk with your policy details for approval before admission.

Yes, an itemized bill is provided at the time of discharge. It includes consultation, room charges, medicines, procedures, and diagnostics for complete transparency.

In select cases, the hospital allows part payments or installment options. Please contact the billing department for eligibility and terms.

Yes, GST-compliant invoices are available upon request. You can use them for insurance claims, company reimbursements, or tax purposes.

Booking an appointment at our hospital is simple and convenient. You can schedule an appointment in three ways:

1. Online: Through our hospital website or mobile app, where you can view doctor profiles, check available timings, and select your preferred slot.
2. Phone: By calling our reception desk during working hours and speaking with our staff for booking assistance.
3. In-person: By visiting the hospital reception and booking directly.

For emergency cases, no prior appointment is required, and patients can directly visit the emergency department.

Yes, appointments can be rescheduled or cancelled if you are unable to attend.

You can do this by contacting the hospital reception or through our online booking system. We recommend informing us at least 24 hours in advance so that the slot can be given to another patient. In case of emergencies, our staff will help you rebook at the earliest available time.

Your personal data is safeguarded through multiple layers of protection. All sensitive information is stored in secure servers with encryption. Our hospital complies with healthcare privacy regulations to ensure your records are safe from unauthorized access. Only authorized medical staff can view your data when necessary for treatment.

Access to your medical records is strictly limited. Only doctors, nurses, and medical staff directly involved in your treatment are permitted to review your information. Administrative staff may access limited details when required for billing or insurance purposes, but they do not have access to full clinical records.

Yes, you can request a copy of your medical records by submitting a written application to our medical records department. For security, you will need to provide a valid photo ID and, in some cases, written consent. Copies may be provided in printed or digital format depending on your preference.

We do not share your personal health information with third parties without your consent. However, in certain cases, limited information may be shared with your insurance provider, government health authorities, or as required by law. Every data exchange follows strict confidentiality and legal compliance protocols.
